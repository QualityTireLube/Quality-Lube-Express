Careers at Quality Tire & Auto Service

We're always looking for motivated individuals ready to grow in the automotive industry. We focus on quality workmanship, honest service, and a strong team environment — whether you're just starting out or bringing years of experience.

Current Openings

Service Advisor

Responsibilities

  • Greet customers and understand vehicle concerns
  • Write repair orders and communicate service recommendations
  • Provide accurate estimates and explain services clearly
  • Coordinate workflow between technicians and customers
  • Maintain strong customer relationships

Expectations

  • Strong communication and customer service skills
  • Ability to multitask in a fast-paced environment
  • Basic automotive knowledge preferred
  • Organized and detail-oriented

Front Counter Assistant

Responsibilities

  • Assist with customer check-in and scheduling
  • Answer phones and direct customer questions
  • Help maintain organized service records
  • Support service advisors with administrative tasks

Expectations

  • Friendly and professional attitude
  • Strong communication skills
  • Reliable and punctual
  • Basic computer and phone skills

Tire / Lube Technician

Responsibilities

  • Perform oil changes and basic maintenance services
  • Mount and balance tires
  • Inspect vehicles and report findings
  • Maintain a clean and safe work environment

Expectations

  • Mechanical aptitude
  • Ability to work efficiently in a team environment
  • Dependable and motivated to learn
  • Entry-level experience welcome

Service Technician

Responsibilities

  • Diagnose and repair vehicle issues
  • Perform routine maintenance and mechanical repairs
  • Use diagnostic equipment and service tools
  • Follow company procedures and quality standards

Expectations

  • Prior automotive repair experience
  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Ability to work independently and efficiently
  • ASE certifications preferred

Master Technician

Responsibilities

  • Perform advanced diagnostics and complex repairs
  • Mentor and assist other technicians
  • Ensure work meets quality and safety standards
  • Handle complex drivability and electrical issues

Expectations

  • Extensive diagnostic experience
  • ASE certifications or equivalent experience
  • Leadership and mentorship ability
  • Commitment to high-quality workmanship
